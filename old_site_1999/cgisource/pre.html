You can sort the list by any of the fields by clicking on the field name (e.g., click on "Year" to sort by their year.)

The room numbers contain three pieces of information. The first letter indicates which section of the hall the room is in: Hayden, Monroe, or Wood (see the hall map). The number is the number of the room. The trailing letter indicates the size of the room; A's are the largest and D's are the smallest.

If you click on the "Alum" field, the list will be displayed with current residents first, followed by alumnae of the hall.
